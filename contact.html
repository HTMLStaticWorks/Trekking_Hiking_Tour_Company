Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for treks?

Most of our beginner treks have a minimum age of 12. Advanced treks require participants to be at least 18 years old.

Do you provide trekking gear?

Yes, we provide essential safety gear. Personal items like boots and clothing can be rented for an additional fee.

What happens in case of bad weather?

Safety is our priority. If weather conditions are dangerous, we will reschedule or offer a full refund.
