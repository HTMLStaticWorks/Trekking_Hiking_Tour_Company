Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our services? Find your answers here.

What is included in the premium trekking package?

Our premium packages include professional certified guides, high-quality gear (tents, sleeping bags, and safety equipment), gourmet meals, permits, local transport, and emergency medical support including oxygen cylinders and satellite phones.

Do I need previous climbing experience for mountain trekking?

We cater to all skill levels. Our trekking itineraries are marked with difficulty ratings. While beginner trails require no prior experience (just basic physical fitness), advanced high-altitude treks require technical climbing training, which we can provide before the expedition.

How do you ensure safety on high-altitude expeditions?

Safety is our top priority. We maintain a 1:3 guide-to-client ratio, carry satellite communication gear, monitor vital signs daily for acute mountain sickness (AMS), and have a guaranteed helicopter evacuation plan in place for all high-altitude routes.

Can we customize our group itinerary?

Yes, absolutely! Through our Custom Travel Planning service, we can tailor the route, duration, accommodation style, and difficulty level to suit the specific needs and goals of your group.
